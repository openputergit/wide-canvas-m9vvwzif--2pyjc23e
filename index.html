Discover Spiti Valley

Nestled in the northeastern part of Himachal Pradesh, Spiti Valley is a cold desert mountain valley located high in the Himalayan mountains. The name "Spiti" means "The Middle Land" - the land between Tibet and India.

With an average altitude of around 12,500 feet above sea level, Spiti Valley offers breathtaking landscapes, ancient monasteries, and a unique cultural experience that will leave you mesmerized.
